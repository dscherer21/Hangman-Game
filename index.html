Hangman

Instructions

Press any key to start!

  • Type a letter into the text box and click the "Guess" button.

  • If the letter you guessed is in the phrase, it will show inside the blanks for each instance of that letter.

  • If you make a wrong guess, your life total will decrease by one.

  • You have 8 wrong guesses before you get "Game Over".

  • You may click the "Reset" button at any time to restart with a new word.
